Our story

Ponnaloy was born from a simple idea: online shopping should feel as good as unboxing something special. Every pixel, every interaction, every product page is crafted to bring that feeling to life.

We combine modern design with practical engineering — Express + SQLite on the backend, polished vanilla JS on the frontend — to deliver a store that's both beautiful and functional.
